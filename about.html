About Venus Handicrafts

Bringing Pakistan's timeless traditions to your home with handmade excellence.

Our Story

Venus Handicrafts began as a small family initiative to preserve the beauty of traditional handicrafts. Over the decades, our skilled artisans have created exquisite pieces cherished worldwide.

Our Mission

To promote Pakistani craftsmanship globally while supporting local artisans. Every product we create reflects cultural heritage and artistic excellence.

What We Offer

Iron, Brass, Wood, Glass, Aluminium handicrafts, Home Décor, Candle Accessories, Tables wares, and many more unique creations.

Why Choose Us

Originality, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction are at the heart of our work. Each product is handmade with passion and care.
